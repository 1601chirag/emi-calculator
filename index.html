EMI Calculator

This EMI calculator helps you calculate your monthly loan EMI quickly and easily. It is useful for home loans, car loans, education loans, and personal loans.

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed amount you pay every month towards repaying a loan, which includes both principal and interest.

Calculate Loan EMI










How EMI is Calculated

EMI is calculated using the following formula:

EMI = P × R × (1 + R)N / ((1 + R)N − 1)

Where:

  • P = Loan amount
  • R = Monthly interest rate
  • N = Number of monthly installments

Example of EMI Calculation

If the loan amount is ₹5,00,000, the annual interest rate is 10%, and the loan tenure is 5 years:

Monthly EMI will be approximately ₹10,624.
